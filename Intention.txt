Environment app Starts in Mackenzie quad.
select one of 5 environments which lead to minigames.

Block 1 selection triggers initiation of mini-game: "Get to first Sysc 1005 Lab";
Block 2 selection triggers initiation of mini-game: "Phisics 1004 -- pure magic";
Block 4 selection triggers initiation of mini-game: "ECOR1010 Midterm --SCE past and future";
Selecting "The Thing" (structure in middle of quad) triggers "Climbing The Thing" minigame;


Block 3 can only be selected after all other games have been completed. With
money collected from other environments you buy a sandwich and coffee and chill
with Leos squad.
